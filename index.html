Software Engineering. Data Science. AI/ML. Product Management. Entrepreneurship.

P-ai is building the modern incubator for CS projects and ideas—providing opportunities in pre-professional software education at the 5C Claremont College Consortium. We enable students to engage on-campus with the ever-expanding field of computing: featuring interdisciplinary projects in tech, finance, health, science, and more.

Our core initiative is Project Teams: every semester, student and industry leaders choose to lead self-designed project teams of their own choosing as Product Managers (PMs). Teams fall in one of our two divisions: SWE or DS/AI. P-ai club members can then join specific teams as Project Members, working together with their group throughout the semester to build something technical.

Our Mission

P-ai also hosts general club workshops, panels, and events to develop a professional software skillset and examine the latest research/applications and their respective ethical implications, in which students will acquire a breadth of knowledge beyond enhancing technical competencies.

P-ai will strive to establish a culture of proactive curiosity-driven learning in addition to practicing interdisciplinary communication. Ultimately, P-ai’s approach will offer a framework of informed decision-making that is beneficial to all careers.

You don’t need CS Experience to join the club! The club’s General Membership is open to all - sign up for our mailing list so you can get info on our events and workshops.
